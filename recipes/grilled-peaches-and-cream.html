Grilled Peaches and Cream

Description

This is an easy grilled peach dessert recipe! It is an elegant, yet simple recipe with only four ingredients! It is perfect for the summer day and has a delicious taste. It is a new way to eat peach in an elegant and exquisite manner. This recipe brings out the natural flavors of a peach with its sweet but tart taste.

Ingredients

  • 4 peaches, halved and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons clover honey
  • 1 cup soft cream cheese with honey and nuts
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Preheat a grill for medium high heat
  2. Brush peaches with a light coating of oil. Place pit side down onto the grill. Grill for 5 minutes, or until the surfaces have nice grill marks. Turn the peaches over, and drizzle with a bit of honey.
  3. Place a dollop of the cream cheese spread in the place where the pit was. Grill for 2 to 3 more minutes, or until the filling is warm.
  4. Serve immediately.
